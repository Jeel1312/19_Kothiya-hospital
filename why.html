Why Choose Us

Emergency

We have highly experienced and qualified personnel to manage our emergency assistance services 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. We have a 24 hours Command and Control Center manned by Doctors to provide advice and assistance to those in need.

Perioperative Care

The goal is to provide better conditions for patients before operation, during operation, and after operation. Patient expectations and concerns are practically addressed. Quality assurance endeavors is also integrated into these perioperative phases so that optimal care of the surgical patient is ensured.

Latest Technology

We have advanced technology based fully computerized laboratory having departments of Biochemistry, Pathology, Microbiology, Immunology, Histopathology, etc. supported by Dr. Lal Path Labs. We have a 300 mA X-Ray machine with a Digital CR LASER system to digitally print our X-rays for a sharper and a long lasting print. We integrates evidence based internationally benchmarked medical practices with modern technology while delivering the highest-quality patient care.

Competitive Price

We aim to provide best possible medical care in the lowest cost possible. We have low competitive price and best quality assured. We have various packages for various surgeries and offer best yet economical medical treatments.

Quality Standards

We strive to provide the patients with the best possible yet economical care and treatment. In essence, total patient care is the drive that propels us ahead on the path of bringing joy on the faces of people in need.

Specialized Doctors

We have experts on staff that are nationally certified and have received advanced training in a variety of specialty areas. Our specialists are up-to-date with the most current treatment programs and technologies, which means we are able to provide the best care for a wide range of specific illnesses and conditions.
